Commercial Property

Our Property Lawyers represents an array of clients including well known high street names, art galleries, catering and retail businesses, high net worth individuals, property investment companies and developers. We provide clients with pragmatic commercial advice across a broad spectrum of property issues.

Our clients value our resources, strength and delivery of the services we provide. We are not conveyancers that handle volume work, but property solicitors, that offer concise and experienced advice.

Our team’s objective is to help our clients meet their goals and complete their transactions smoothly and efficiently to create long lasting relationships.

Services we offer include:

  • Commercial Lease renewals and negotiation
  • Landlord and tenant disputes
  • Retail franchising
  • Lease Assignments
  • Review of tenancy agreements, service contracts and long leases
  • Breaking of the above, new terms and renewals & service charge enquiries
  • Trust Deeds, charges, personal guarantees and company ownerships
  • Licensing

Where possible we offer fixed and capped fees to enable client's to manage their budget.

We keep you informed daily of your matter's progress and assign you with a dedicated team of solicitors and trainee to manage your enquiries.
