Tax Law

Tax is a core part of our business law practice and has been for decades. The FRIEDMAN LLP Law Firm tax team tax team provides creative yet practical tax advice to a wide range of clients including public and private companies, private equity funds, partnerships, pension funds, investment advisors, governmental entities, owner-managed businesses and high net worth individuals.

Our tax lawyers have received high recognition from The Best Lawyers® in Canada directory, The Canadian Legal Lexpert® directory, Martindale Hubbell® Peer Review Ratings™, International Tax Review® and Legal 500®.

The depth of the FRIEDMAN LLP Law Firm tax team is extended with our World Services Group affiliation which provides us with quick and efficient access to tax professionals in over 130 countries. We maintain regular contact with the tax groups at each of the Canadian law firms in the World Services Group, and as a result, have direct access to the expertise of over 60 tax lawyers across Canada.

Tax considerations are key drivers to many business decisions and transactions. Our tax team can provide you with the advice that you need to make those decisions.
